# Attitude System

## Attitude Range

Attitude ranges from **-5** (hostile nemesis, may attack on sight) to **+5** (fanatical love and adoration).

## Initial Attitude

GM determines initial attitude based on:
- Race
- Appearance
- Social class
- Rank
- Prior reputation

**First meeting typical range:** -2 to +2
- +2: Friendly disposition
- -2: Unfriendly disposition
- 0: Neutral/indifferent

## Attitude Progression

- First interaction can shift to -3/+3 range
- Progressing further requires significant actions or stakes
- Roleplay and actions affect attitude naturally

## Attitude Effects on Favors

| Attitude | Cost Modifier |
|----------|---------------|
| -5 | Triple the cost |
| 0 | +20% markup |
| +3 | Half price or IOU |
| +5 | Free (fanatical devotion) |
