Conditions in Exceed apply bonuses, penalties and ongoing effects. 

![[Bonus Types]]



All types of conditions range from max +3 to min -3.

Most conditions use the default 3 duration presets from the effects and abilities that apply them. This is written in natural language, but follows these basic presets.
**1. One-Time Use** (next qualifying action)
- Advantage/Disadvantage from luck, or pre-rolls from #prediction abilities.
- Off-Guard from faint.
**2. End of Turn** (fades at turn end)
- Shaken from Demoralize
- Blinded from Flash
**3. Until Removed** (The source should specify removal conditions)
- Grace +2 while under effect of Ally's limit spell. 
- Off-guard while flanked
- Grabbed while grapple maintained (removed by grappler's release or Escape action)
- Dazzled until clears eyes with a DC 10 DX check.
- Shaken 2 while in Fear Aura. (leaving aura)
- Focus -2 from a combat maneuver requiring a first aid or a breather. 

# Conditions are to be revamped in MS6.
# 1. Enhancement Conditions


- **Grace X:** +-X to Agility (AG) and Dexterity (DX) based rolls.
- **Vigor X:** +-X to Might (MG) and Endurance (EN) based rolls.
- **Focus X:** +-X to Willpower (WL) and Perception (PR) based rolls.
- **Sharpness X:** +-X to Charisma (CH) and Wit (WT) based rolls.
- **Quickened X**:  +-X actions at the start of each turn.

# 3. Morale Conditions

This penalty affects a character's ability to perform certain types of actions, representing fear, doubt, or a lack of confidence.

- **Shaken X:** X morale penalty on all rolls.
- **Inspired** X: X morale bonus on all rolls.

# 3. Luck
* Jinxed - next roll of a specific type or simply the next roll is rolled with disadvantage.
* Blessed - next roll of a specific type or simply the next roll is rolled with an advantage.

# **Musings**

**Stunned X** : Lose X actions next turn, cannot use reactions

**Bleed** X- At the end of your turn take X damage . 

**Fragile** X - Take X extra damage from each attack.

**Pinned** (Bow specialization) X - Spend an action to remove the arrow or get bleed X when you move next time. (this is almost ranged stunned, but weaker)

**Bleeding:** Bleeding  equal to highest wound severity level (0-3 damage per turn until treated).//remove
