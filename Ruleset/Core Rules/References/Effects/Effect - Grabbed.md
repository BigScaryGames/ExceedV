You are held in place by another creature through a grapple.

**Tags:** #Situational #Condition

Grants the following conditions:
- [[Condition - Off Guard]]
- [[Condition - Immobilized]]

And the following penalties:
- Physical actions are rolled at a disadvantage
- Cannot use weapons with Reach against the grappler

Grappler can release as a free action. Grappled creature can use [[Escape]] to break free or [[Grapple]] to reverse the grapple. [[Trip]] maneuver will drop both of you to the ground.
