Gain additional HP based on your current Max_Wounds.

This effect is granted by leveled conditioning perks (Poison Resistance, Waterfall Training, Mental Resilience, Cold Conditioning, Heat Conditioning, Battle Scarred, and Magical Conditioning).

**Progression:**
- **Levels 1-4:** Gain +1 HP per level
- **Level 5:** Consolidates into +1 Max_Wounds (the new wound grants full armor and endurance bonuses)
