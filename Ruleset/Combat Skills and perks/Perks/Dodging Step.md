# Dodging Step

 **Requirement:** AG 2
 **Attributes:** AG/PR
 **AP Cost:** 2
 **XP Cost:** 5
 **Tags:** #Universal

## Short Description
Improve your dodge by taking a tactical step.

## Effect
When dodging, make a step and take +1 to dodge. If it is against a melee strike, dodge within attacker's reach if possible.
