 **Requirement:** SH2
 **Attributes:** PR/AG+SH
 **AP Cost:** 2
 **XP Cost:** 10
 **Tags:** #Shield

## Short Description
Block attacks targeting adjacent allies instead of them.

## Effect
Trigger: Adjacent ally is attacked. Use your reaction to attempt block instead of them. If This is an AOE effect, you protect both of you.
