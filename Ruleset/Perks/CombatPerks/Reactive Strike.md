# Reactive Strike

 **Requirement:** AG 2+MD2
 **Attributes:** AG/PR+MD
 **AP Cost:** 2
 **XP Cost:** 10
 **Tags:** #Universal

## Short Description
Strike enemies as they retreat from your reach.

## Effect
When an enemy strides or runs out of your reach - make a free strike as a reaction before they leave their space.
