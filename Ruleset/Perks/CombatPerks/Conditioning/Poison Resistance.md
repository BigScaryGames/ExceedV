## Description
Through careful, measured exposure to various toxins under medical supervision, you train your body to resist poisons. Starting with mild irritants and building tolerance over time, you develop both physical resilience and the ability to identify toxins through sensory examination. At the peak of this conditioning, your body has adapted to neutralize most common poisons with remarkable efficiency.
