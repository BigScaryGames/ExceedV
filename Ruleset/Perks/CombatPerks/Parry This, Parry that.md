# Parry This, Parry that

 **Requirement:** MD 3, Projectile parry
 **Attributes:** AG/DX +MD
 **AP Cost:** 2
 **XP Cost:** 10
 **Tags:** #Universal

## Short Description
Parry magical effects and protect those behind you.

## Effect
You can parry Lines, Rays, Bursts and cones on a success - targets behind you are unaffected.
