# Thrown Training

**Requirements:** -
**Attributes:**  DX or AG
**Cost:** 5 XP
**AP Cost:** -
**Tags:** #Combat #WeaponTraining 

## Short Description
Like a kite in the sky, you were born to move with wind.
## Effect
* Skirmisher's strike (Activity). Once per turn you can step back as a part of the thrown attack. 
## Description
The whole point of thrown weapons is to be away from the enemy just enough not to get killed. You have learned the basics.
