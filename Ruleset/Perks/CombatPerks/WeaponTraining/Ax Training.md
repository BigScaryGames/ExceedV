# Ax Training

**Requirements:** -
**Attributes:**  MG or EN
**Cost:** 5 XP
**AP Cost:** -
**Tags:** #Combat #WeaponTraining 

## Short Description
Ax in your hands is a weapon of block destruction.
## Effect
* When attacking with axe, and defense was declared as blocking (be it with shield, natural resilience or magical barrier) before the roll, spend your reaction to gain +2 Situational bonus to hit.
## Description
Ax in your hands is a weapon of block destruction. Be it magic or thick skin you have the best weapon to cleave through such an obstacle.
