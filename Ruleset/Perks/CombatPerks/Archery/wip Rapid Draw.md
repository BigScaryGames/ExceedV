# Rapid Draw

**Requirements:** Tier 4, [[Fast Archer]]
**Attributes:** AG/DX
**Cost:** 10 XP
**AP Cost:** 2 AP
**Tags:** #Combat

## Short Description
Light-draw shots cost 2 AP but deal 1 fewer damage die.

## Grants

![[Ability - Rapid Draw]]

## Description
You've mastered the technique of quickly drawing and releasing arrows with a lighter pull, sacrificing raw power for speed. This technique excels when applying poison, leveraging damage buffs, or simply overwhelming enemies with volume of fire.
