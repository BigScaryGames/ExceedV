# Hair Trigger

**Requirements:** Tier 2 
**Attributes:** PR/AG
**Cost:** 10 XP
**AP Cost:** Variable (readied action)
**Tags:** #Combat

## Short Description
Make a readied strike at the first creature entering range/sight without spending a Reaction.

# Grants
Ability - Hair trigger
Ready a bow strike with a trigger of target entering your line of sight or target entering your reach or both. When any hostile target qualifies roll for an attack.
You don't spend your reaction to make this strike.
## Description
You keep an arrow nocked and ready, prepared to loose it the instant a target appears.
When the trigger fires, you automatically shoot the first qualifying creature without spending a Reaction.
