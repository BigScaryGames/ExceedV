# Follow-Up Strike

**Requirements:** 1H2
**Attributes:** AG/DX+1H
**Cost:** 5 XP
**AP Cost:** 2
**Tags:** #Combat #OneHanded

## Short Description
Reduce AP cost for consecutive attacks on same target.

## Effect
After hitting a target, your next attack this turn against the same target costs -1 AP.
