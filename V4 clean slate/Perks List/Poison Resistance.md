# Poison Resistance

**Requirements:** Medicine 1  
**Attribute:** Endurance  
**Cost:** 5 CP  

## Description
Through constant exposure to small doses, you have developed resistance to poisons and can identify them safely.

## Mechanics
- +2 bonus to all Endure saves against poisons
- Can identify poisons by smell or taste without risk of harm
- Resistance applies to both natural and crafted poisons
- Does not protect against magical toxins unless specifically noted
- Can safely handle poisonous substances for identification purposes
