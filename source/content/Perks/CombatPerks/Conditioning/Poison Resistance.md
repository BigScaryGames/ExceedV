## Description
Through systematic exposure to toxins, venoms, and poisons, your body has developed remarkable resistance. Whether you are an alchemist testing your creations, a spy trained to resist interrogation drugs, or simply someone who believes in building immunity through controlled exposure, you have learned to shrug off substances that would incapacitate others. Your liver processes toxins efficiently, and your immune system neutralizes threats before they can take hold. At mastery, common poisons become mere inconveniences, and even deadly venoms require time to affect you.
