## Description
Through training beneath waterfalls, in raging rapids, or against powerful currents, your body has adapted to function under extreme water pressure and flow. You have learned to breathe efficiently, maintain position against crushing forces, and move with fluid grace even when water threatens to sweep you away. Whether you are a monk seeking enlightenment under mountain cascades, a river guide navigating treacherous whitewater, or a coastal warrior training in tidal zones, you have developed an aquatic resilience that few can match. At mastery, you move through water as others move through air.
