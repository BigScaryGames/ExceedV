## Description
Through meditation, prayer, philosophical study, or mental conditioning, you have built remarkable psychological resilience. Your mind is a fortress, capable of withstanding fear, doubt, and psychic intrusion. Whether you are a monk who has spent years in silent contemplation, a scholar who has plumbed the depths of forbidden knowledge, or a soldier trained to resist interrogation and torture, you have learned to maintain your sense of self against forces that would shatter weaker minds. At mastery, even direct psychic assault finds little purchase against your iron will.
