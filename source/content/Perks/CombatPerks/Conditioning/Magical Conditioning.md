## Description
Through repeated spellcasting, magical meditation, or exposure to raw arcane energies, your body has adapted to channel magical forces with increasing efficiency. You have learned to regulate the flow of power through your channels, prevent magical burnout, and maintain concentration when wild energies threaten to overwhelm your control. Whether you are a wizard who has spent decades studying the arcane, a sorcerer whose bloodline grants natural affinity, or a cleric whose faith has been tested through countless miracles, your tolerance for magical exertion has been forged through practice. At mastery, you channel energies that would exhaust other casters.
