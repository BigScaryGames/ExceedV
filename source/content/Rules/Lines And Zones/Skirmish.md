A group in a zone that are in melee range with each other. A single character can't be in 2 engagements at the same time.

## Strength in numbers 
Unless a zone states otherwise having a numerical disadvantage within a skirmish provides -1 to deflect and dodge rolls per difference to the defender capped at -4.

Reach and Range weapons allow attacking without entering the skirmish, the attacks count the attacker for the benefit of numeric advantage for that attack.

To disengage from the [[Skirmish]] you need to perform a [[Disengage]] action. Trying to Leave the [[Skirmish]] with or without a [[Disengage]] action can interact with an ability of an effect.
