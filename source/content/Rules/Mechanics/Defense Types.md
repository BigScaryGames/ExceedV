There are 4 main defensive options in the game and they rely on equipment and perks.
* Deflect - Whether blocking with a shield or parrying with a weapon, you redirect the threat away from harming you.
* Dodge - for cases when moving out of the harm's way sounds optimal.
* Resolve - for when your willpower your 'self' are under attack.
* Endure - for when your body is under pressure or exertion, poison or disease.


| Defense     | Formula                                                                          |
| ----------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Deflect** | Martial + Agility/Dexterity/Might(based on weapon type)+ Equipment Defense bonus |
| **Dodge**   | Agility + Perception                                                             |
| **Resolve** | Will+Charisma                                                                    |
| **Endure**  | Endurance + Might                                                                |
## Usage by Attack Trait

| Trait           | Valid Defenses                               | Examples                                           |
| --------------- | -------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **#Strike**     | Deflect, Dodge                               | Melee attacks, touch spells                        |
| **#Projectile** | Dodge, Deflect with a shield, or with perks. | Arrows, thrown weapons, targeted ranged spells     |
| **#Burst**      | Dodge (Deflect with perks)                   | Explosions, Burst spells                           |
| **#Mind**       | Resolve                                      | Fear, charm, mind control, mental distortion       |
| **#Body**       | Endure                                       | Poison, disease, exhaustion, body-affecting curses |
