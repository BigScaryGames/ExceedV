## Attacking and Defending

All offensive effects have one or more traits of #Strike, #Projectile, #Burst, #Mind, #Body. Those determine which defenses can be used against them.

### Ranged Combat

![[Ranged Combat]]

**Unopposed:** Roll defense against a static DC (traps, environmental hazards, ongoing effects)
**Opposed:** Roll defense against attacker's roll (creature attacks, targeted spells)

Attack and defense rolls follow standard check rules from [[2. Core Resolution System]]: 2d10 + Skill + Attribute (+ Bonuses/Penalties).
