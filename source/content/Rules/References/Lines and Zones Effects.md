Most Effects are doing one of the followings
Lines
1. Modified cost - keeping the same distance the cost one way or both ways is altered. Example - going uphill, or through mud, or even a spell helping to cross faster for allies.
2. Checks to cross. - You need to succeed a check to cross to the other Zone. (Iced ladders, uphill, etc) - you can use abilities or roll a check to bypass.
3. Hazardous check  - like a check, but provides damage on a failure.
4. Barrier - requires a breaking check or sufficient amount of damage.
5. Enemies - you and your enemies can create all of the above assuming you have the means.

Zones:

1. Defensive advantage - the party that controls the zone against the invaders can have bonuses.(For things like defensive cover)
2. Hazardous zone - e.g. everything is on fire, everyone in the zone is taking damage/rolling endure/resist.
3. Ability modifiers. Zone can have a penalty for all perception rolls - e.g. filled with smoke. Have a sticky floor - agility penalties. Reduced gravity and MANY more.
4. Different magical effects - like healing, buffing team, harming specific types of creatures, etc.
