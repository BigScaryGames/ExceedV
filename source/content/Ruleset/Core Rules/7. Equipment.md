# Weapons

### Weapon Traits

| Trait           | Effect                                                                                                                    |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Finesse**     | Use DX instead of AG for attacks and parries                                                                              |
| **Reach**       | Can target enemies 1-2m away                                                                                              |
| **Long Reach**  | Can target enemies 2-3m away                                                                                              |
| **Range X**     | Can attack to Xm without penalty; beyond, -3 per range increment                                                          |
| **Concealed**   | +2 bonus to hide on person                                                                                                |
| **Conduit**     | Hand counts as free for spellcasting                                                                                      |
| **Double**      | Can deal two types of damage, chosen when attacking                                                                       |
| **Nonlethal**   | Deals nonlethal damage                                                                                                    |
| **Quick Drawn** | Can draw as part of attacking                                                                                             |
| **Large**       | Cannot attack creatures inside your space                                                                                 |
| **Sweeping**    | Each consecutive strike in multi-target abilities deals +1 damage                                                         |
| **Versatile**   | Two-handed use with empty off-hand adds +1 damage. When Versatile weapon is used in 2 hands it uses 1 less AP per strike. |

### Weapon Weight

| Marker     | AP  | Attack Stat | Parry Stat |
| ---------- | --- | ----------- | ---------- |
| Light (L)  | 2   | DX or AG    | DX or AG   |
| Medium (M) | 3   | AG          | AG         |
| Heavy (H)  | 4   | MG          | AG         |
### Weapon Categories

Having training in any listed category allows you to use the weapon without the -2 untrained penalty.

### Ranged Weapons

All bows have a base strike cost of 3 AP.

**Range Increments:** For thrown weapons, range increment equals Range value. For bows with Aim X, range increment equals X.

| Weapon      | Category | Damage        | Might Req | Range | Traits |
| ----------- | -------- | ------------- | --------- | ----- | ------ |
| Shortbow    | Bows     | 4 + Might Req | 0-5       | 10m   | —      |
| Longbow     | Bows     | 5 + Might Req | 1-5       | 20m   | Large  |
| Recurve Bow | Bows     | 4 + Might     | 0-1       | 40m   | —      |

**Bow Might Requirements:**
- Below minimum Might: -1 to hit per point of deficit
- Meeting or exceeding Might Req: Add Might Req to damage

**Armor Penalty X/Y:** X = penalty when not meeting Might Req; Y = penalty when meeting Might Req. Penalty applies to Speed (meters per action) and Dodge rolls.
### **MS5 Thumb rule**. on masterwork rules
High quality variants cost 2x-5x of the armor cost and provide extra armor bonus up to GM (+1/+2/tbd).

**Shield Block Stats:**
- Light shields use Agility
- Medium shields use Endurance
- Heavy shields use Might

**Armor Penalty:** Combines with worn armor. Defense bonus applies to all mundane defenses; magical shields also apply to spells.
