# First Aid

**AP Cost:** 4
**Traits:** #Interact #Healing

**Roll:** Medicine skill vs Treatment DC

Restore HP and treat conditions.

- **Trained:** 4 actions to perform
- **Untrained:** Can be performed in 3 rounds, but cannot be done during combat

*Note: Exiting combat to perform first aid prevents dodging, blocking, and parrying. Taking a defensive action causes the first aid to fail.*

**Enhanced by:** [[First Responder]]
