**AP Cost:** 2-4 (based on weapon weight)
**Traits:** #Attack #Projectile

**Roll:** Martial + Dexterity/Perception vs Distance DC, then vs defense

Attack a target at range with a ranged weapon. The attack must first beat the Distance DC based on range increment (see [[Ranged Combat]]), then the defender rolls Block or Dodge against your result (or other available defenses).
