# Trading Rules

Most items have a listed price in the rulebook, which is the market selling price.

## Base Prices

| Transaction | Price Range |
|-------------|-------------|
| Shops sell at | 100% of listed price |
| Shops buy at | 50% of listed price |

## Bargaining

Bargaining and attitude shift the ranges:
- **Buying:** 75-125% of listed price
- **Selling:** 25-75% of listed price
- **Modifier:** Â±5% per attitude step

## Bargaining Rolls

Roll a social skill to shift the attitude for this purchase by 1 step:

| Purchase Size | Skill to Use |
|---------------|--------------|
| Small | Fast Talk |
| Large | Negotiations or Manipulation |

Roll against seller's social skill:
- **Success:** Treat bargain as 1 step better
- **Failure:** Treat bargain as 1 step worse
